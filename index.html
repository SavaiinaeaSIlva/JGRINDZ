About JGRINDZ

Welcome to JGRINDZ, your go-to spot for authentic Polynesian cuisine in the heart of Hilo. Founded by Jeffrey Silva, our mission is to bring the rich, vibrant flavors of the Pacific Islands to your plate, using traditional recipes and the freshest local ingredients.

From savory kalua pig to refreshing poke bowls and sweet haupia, every dish is prepared with aloha and a deep respect for our heritage. We believe food is more than just sustenance; it's a celebration of culture, family, and community. Come and experience a true taste of the islands with us!

We are committed to providing a memorable dining experience that transports you straight to the shores of Polynesia.
